MORE ABOUT OUR ORGANISATION

BACKGROUND

Ingqwele Community Development Organization was established and began operating since January 2016 at Umlazi F section. It provides with extra classes for Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Accounting and Life Sciences. It promotes social responsibility programs for youth through dialogues and donates sanitary towels and school uniforms to children who are orphans. Ingqwele also provide awareness programs on gender based violence.


OUR VISION

  • To be recognized as South Africa’s leading youth academy and to form programmes that will encourage the youth to learn and to see the vitality of education.
  • To see orphans living in the comfortable and conducive environment that enables them to pursue their career path
  • The organization is called to develop the youth by implementing effective youth development programmes

MISSION

To produce students who pass with high levels so that they do not encounter any hindrances when they apply for bursaries and also inspire them to be responsible, resilient and to equip youth with the necessary skills to confront the forth industrial revolution.
